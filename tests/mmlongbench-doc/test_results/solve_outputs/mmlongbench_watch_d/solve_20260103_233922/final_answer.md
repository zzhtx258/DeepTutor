## S1: Steps to Customize the Function of the Down Button on HUAWEI Watch

To customize the function of the Down button on your HUAWEI watch, you need to follow a total of **four steps**. These steps are clearly outlined as follows:

1. **Press the Up button**: This action opens the app list on your HUAWEI watch.
2. **Navigate to Settings**: Scroll through the app list and select the **Settings** option.
3. **Select Down Button**: Within the Settings menu, find and select the **Down button** option.
4. **Choose an app and customize the function**: From the available options, select the app you want the Down button to open and confirm your selection. Finally, return to the home screen to utilize the Down button with its new settings.

By following these steps, you can effectively customize the functionality of the Down button to suit your preferences.
