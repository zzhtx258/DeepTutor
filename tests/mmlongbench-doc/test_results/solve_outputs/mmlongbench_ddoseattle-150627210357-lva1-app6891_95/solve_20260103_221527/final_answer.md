In the context of the Analytics Value Chain, the stage that comes between Reporting and Action is **Decision Making**. This critical phase involves leveraging the insights generated from the reporting process to inform strategic decisions.

The Decision Making stage serves as a bridge, where stakeholders analyze the reports and determine the best course of action based on the insights provided. This ensures that the actions taken are informed and aligned with organizational goals, ultimately driving value from the analytics process.

In summary, the flow from Reporting to Action is facilitated by the Decision Making stage, underscoring the importance of informed choices in the analytics workflow.
