9. **Indian Institute of Space Science and Technology (IIST)**: IIST provides education and research opportunities in space science and technology, fostering the next generation of space scientists and engineers.

10. **National Remote Sensing Centre (NRSC)**: NRSC specializes in remote sensing satellite data and its applications, supporting various sectors such as agriculture, forestry, and urban planning.
