## S1: What Does NARL Stand For?

NARL stands for **National Atmospheric Research Laboratory**. This institution is situated in Gadanki, near Tirupati, India, and operates as an autonomous society under the auspices of the Department of Space (DOS) in India. The primary focus of NARL is to conduct atmospheric research, which encompasses both observational studies and modeling efforts aimed at understanding atmospheric phenomena and improving weather forecasting capabilities.

The establishment of NARL highlights India's commitment to advancing atmospheric science and enhancing its research capabilities in this critical area. By leveraging advanced technologies and methodologies, NARL plays a significant role in contributing to the global understanding of atmospheric processes.
